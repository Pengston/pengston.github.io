🚚 Kei Delivery

Your tiny truck. Your delivery empire.

About the Game

Kei Delivery is a business simulation and delivery game set in a cozy, rural Japanese town. Start with a humble kei truck and grow your one-person delivery hustle into a logistics empire.

Features

  • 📦 Real-time package delivery
  • 🛠️ Customize and maintain your kei trucks
  • 🏪 Grow your own delivery business
  • 🌦️ Dynamic weather and traffic
  • 🏙️ Expand from a small town to big city districts
